Who We Are

Defend Against Covid-19 is an independent population health research center located in Greece that provides rigorous and comparable measurement of the world's most important health problems around the virus Covid-19 and evaluates the strategies used to address them. Defend Against COVID-19 makes this information freely available so that policymakers have the evidence they need to make informed decisions about how to allocate resources to best improve population health.


Defend Against Covid-19 aspires to make available to the world high-quality information on population health, its determinants, and the performance of health systems.

What we do

To help people live longer lives in better health worldwide, we work with a broad network of researchers, statisticians, and policymakers, following a science-based, collaborative approach to defend against Covid-19.

Knowledge Sharing

Because health improvement often starts from within communities, we invite the public to help us interpret our results and advance health metrics and evaluation through reports, policy discussions, data visualizations, and other activities.

Data Relevance

To better guide health policy, we make our findings comparable across time and populations using the most recent data available, and we provide information at the most local area of measurement possible.
